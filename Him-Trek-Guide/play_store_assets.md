## 📝 Store Listing

### App Name
**TrekShield**

### Short Description
**Your safety partner when the network fails.**

### Full Description
```text
TrekShield is a safety app designed for trekkers and outdoor explorers.

It works even without internet by using SMS to send your location to a trusted contact if you fail to check in.

Key Features:
• Offline safety tracking
• Automatic emergency SMS alerts
• Simple timer-based check-in system
• Low battery usage

Why SMS?
In remote areas where internet is unavailable, TrekShield uses standard SMS to ensure your location can still be shared in emergencies.

Your privacy matters:
We do not read your messages or share your data with third parties.
```

## 🛡️ SMS Permission Declaration

### ❓ Why do you need SMS permission?
```text
TrekShield uses SMS permission to send emergency alerts to a user-selected contact if the user fails to check in during a trek.

This functionality is essential because the app is designed for remote areas where internet connectivity is unavailable.

Without SMS, the core safety feature of the app would not function.
```

### ❓ Why not use alternatives (e.g. internet messaging)?
```text
Internet-based messaging is unreliable in remote trekking areas. SMS ensures delivery even without data connectivity, making it critical for emergency communication.
```
