TrekShield Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

1. SMS Permissions (SEND_SMS)

The core functionality of TrekShield relies on Android's SMS capabilities. This permission is utilized exclusively as a "dead-man switch" safety mechanism. It automatically dispatches your last known GPS coordinates to your self-designated emergency contact only if you fail to cancel the timer before your trek expires.

TrekShield does not read your personal SMS inbox, nor does it monitor incoming messages.

2. Data Collection and Usage

TrekShield is designed as an offline-first safety utility. We do not sell, rent, or distribute your data to third parties. We collect your Name and chosen Emergency Contact strictly for use in the automated dispatch text.

3. Location Tracking

Location data (GPS) is securely captured locally on your device and synced with our backend infrastructure solely to provide accurate emergency tracking logs for your trusted contacts. Your location is never requested when the app timer is inactive.

4. Contact

If you have questions about this security policy or data operations, please contact the developer directly at ashmitkumarbwj@gmail.com.
