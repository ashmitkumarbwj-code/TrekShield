TrekShield Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

TrekShield is a safety app designed to help users during treks, especially in low or no network conditions.

Information We Collect

  • Name: Provided by you during setup to identify you in emergency messages.
  • Phone Number: Your emergency contact's number to send alerts.
  • Location Data:strong> Captured only during an active trek tracking session.

How We Use Information

TrekShield uses this information solely to:

  • Track the user's location during an active trek.
  • Send automated emergency SMS alerts to your selected contact if you fail to check in.

SMS Permission Usage

TrekShield uses SEND_SMS permission strictly to send emergency alerts to a user-selected contact. The app DOES NOT read, store, or access your personal messages or inbox.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data Storage

Location data is temporarily stored on secure servers to support the live tracking dashboard for your trusted contacts.

User Control

Users can stop tracking anytime by using the "I am Safe" feature in the app, which immediately cancels any scheduled emergency alerts.
